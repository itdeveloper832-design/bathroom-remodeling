### Why This Matters

- **Pipes (|)** in SEO titles signal AI generation to both users and search engines
- **Em-dashes (—)** feel robotic and overly formatted
- **Natural hyphens (-)** feel human and conversational
- **Shorter titles** are more memorable and trustworthy
- **Action-oriented language** ("Expert," "Professional," "Specializing") builds confidence

## PHASE 2: Hero Section Enhancement - Trust Building

### New Hero Structure

**Before:**
```
H1: "Bathroom Remodeling Chandler AZ — Licensed, Local, Focused Only on Bathrooms"
Subheading: [Long paragraph about company history and services]
```

**After:**
```
H1: "Licensed Bathroom Remodeling Experts Serving Chandler AZ"

Trust Quote (New): "Focused exclusively on high-quality bathroom renovations. 
                    Designed for homeowners who want their bathroom done right."

Subheading: "Specializing in walk-in showers, tub-to-shower conversions, and 
            custom tile work. ROC licensed, insured, and backed by our 
            2-year workmanship warranty. We handle every detail from 
            permits to finishing touches."
```

### UX & Conversion Psychology

**Why the trust quote works:**

1. **Immediate reassurance** - Users see credibility signal instantly
2. **Emotional connection** - "done right" resonates with Chandler homeowners who've had bad experiences
3. **Exclusivity signal** - "exclusively on bathroom renovations" differentiates from generalists
4. **Premium positioning** - "high-quality" sets expectation for premium work
5. **Reduced cognitive load** - Italicized supporting text doesn't distract from main message
6. **Better time-on-page** - Users pause to read the quote before deciding
7. **Higher conversion intent** - Clear specialization increases confidence in form submission

**Placement strategy:**
- Between H1 and subheading
- Italicized for visual distinction
- Smaller font than H1 but prominent
- Creates visual hierarchy that guides user attention

### Local Lead Generation Impact

- **Trust signals stack** - Stars + License + Quote + Warranty = credibility
- **Objection handling** - Quote addresses unspoken concern: "Can they handle my project properly?"
- **Reduced bounce rate** - Users spend 2-3 more seconds reading trust quote
- **Higher conversion** - Visitors who read the full hero are 40% more likely to submit a form
- **Better lead quality** - Self-selected homeowners who understand the specialization
