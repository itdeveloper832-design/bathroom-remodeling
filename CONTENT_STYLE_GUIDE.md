# ARZ Home Remodeling - Content Style Guide

## Overview
This guide ensures all website content maintains a premium, human-written voice while adhering to SEO best practices and brand standards.

---

## CRITICAL RULES - DO NOT USE

### ❌ Em-Dashes (—)
**Status**: FORBIDDEN across entire website  
**Reason**: Marks content as AI-generated; reduces perceived authenticity

**Instead of:**
```
"Understanding the schedule—knowing when water will be shut off—helps..."
```

**Use:**
```
"Understanding the schedule, including when water will be shut off, helps..."
```

### ❌ Pipe Separators (|) in Meta Content
**Status**: FORBIDDEN in titles, descriptions, body text  
**Reason**: Generic AI marker; reduces click-through rates

**Instead of:**
```
"Bathroom Remodeling Chandler AZ | Licensed Contractor | Walk-In Showers"
```

**Use:**
```
"Bathroom Remodeling Chandler AZ - Licensed Contractor Specializing in Walk-In Showers"
```

### ❌ Robotic Tone Phrases
**Status**: FORBIDDEN  
**Examples to avoid:**
- "In conclusion..."
- "In this article, we will discuss..."
- "It is important to note that..."
- "This guide covers..."
- "Furthermore..."
- "Ultimately..."

**Use instead:**
- Start directly with the point
- Use conversational language
- "You should know..."
- "Here's what matters..."
- "This is why..."

## APPROVED PUNCTUATION

### ✅ Hyphens (-) for Separators
Use hyphens for separating meta title concepts:
```
"Bathroom Remodeling Chandler AZ - Licensed Contractor Specializing in Walk-In Showers"
"Shower Remodeling Chandler AZ - Expert Installation"
```

### ✅ Commas for Natural Pauses
```
"Walk-in showers, tub-to-shower conversions, and custom tile work..."
```

### ✅ Periods for Clear Breaks
```
"We specialize in walk-in showers. Our team has 15+ years of experience."
```

### ✅ Colons for Lists
```
"Our services include: walk-in showers, tub conversions, tile installation, and vanity work."
```

## CONTENT VOICE & TONE

### Brand Voice Characteristics
- **Professional**: Credible, knowledgeable
- **Approachable**: Friendly, not corporate
- **Honest**: Real benefits, no overselling
- **Focused**: Specialization in bathrooms only
- **Premium**: High-quality work, not budget-focused

### DO's

✅ Write like a trusted expert having a conversation  
✅ Use "we," "you," "your" conversationally  
✅ Start sentences with action words  
✅ Address homeowner concerns directly  
✅ Use specific numbers and guarantees  
✅ Show personality while staying professional  

**Example:**
```
"Most homeowners worry about their bathroom being unusable during the remodel. 
We handle that. Our typical project is complete in 2-3 weeks, and we work 
around your schedule to minimize disruption."
```

### DON'Ts

❌ Avoid passive voice ("The bathroom was renovated...")  
❌ Avoid marketing clichés ("Your dream bathroom awaits!")  
❌ Avoid excessive capitalization (LIKE THIS)  
❌ Avoid ALL CAPS except for acronyms (ROC338304 is OK)  
❌ Avoid exclamation points in professional content  
❌ Avoid AI-style transitions ("Let's dive in...")

## META CONTENT STANDARDS

### Page Titles (SEO Title)
- **Length**: 50-60 characters
- **Format**: `[Service] [Location] - [Key Differentiator]`
- **Examples**:
  - ✅ "Bathroom Remodeling Chandler AZ - Licensed Contractor"
  - ✅ "Walk-In Shower Installation - Expert Design"
  - ❌ "Bathroom Remodeling | Chandler AZ | Licensed | ROC338304"

### Meta Descriptions
- **Length**: 150-160 characters
- **Format**: Benefit + CTA + phone/location
- **Examples**:
  - ✅ "Professional walk-in shower installation in Chandler AZ. Licensed, insured, 2-year warranty. Free estimate: (520) 569-3339."
  - ❌ "Walk-in showers — modern bathroom design — Chandler AZ | Licensed contractor | Call today"

### H1 Tags
- **Per page**: Exactly 1
- **Format**: Direct, benefit-focused
- **Examples**:
  - ✅ "Licensed Bathroom Remodeling Experts Serving Chandler AZ"
  - ❌ "Your Bathroom Remodeling Solution in Chandler, AZ"

## BLOG POST STANDARDS

### Blog Post Structure
1. **H1 Title** - Problem or benefit focused
2. **Opening** - Hook with real concern (2-3 sentences)
3. **Body** - Answer in sections with H2/H3 headers
4. **CTA** - Clear call to action (estimate/phone)

### Blog Content Voice
- Write as if explaining to a neighbor
- Use "I've seen..." and "We find that..."
- Share specific examples from real projects
- Address objections proactively
- Keep paragraphs short (2-3 sentences max)

### Blog Post Example Opening
```
Most homeowners put off bathroom remodeling because they think it requires 
gutting the entire room and living with construction for months. That's 
outdated thinking. Modern bathroom remodels can be completed in 2-3 weeks 
with minimal disruption to your home.

Here's what actually happens during a typical bathroom remodel...
```

## SEO & KEYWORD INTEGRATION

### Keyword Placement - DO's
✅ Use keywords naturally in first 100 words  
✅ Include keywords in H2/H3 headers where relevant  
✅ Use variations and related terms  
✅ Mention service area cities naturally  
✅ Include "Chandler," "Mesa," "Gilbert" where appropriate  

### Keyword Placement - DON'Ts
❌ Keyword stuffing (repeating same phrase)  
❌ Unnatural phrasing to force keywords  
❌ Keywords at the end of sentences artificially  
❌ Forcing "near me" in every sentence  

**Natural keyword example:**
```
"Bathroom remodeling in Chandler isn't just about new tiles. We handle 
walk-in showers, tub-to-shower conversions, and complete master bath 
renovations. Many of our projects in Chandler, Mesa, and Gilbert focus 
on accessibility and modern design."
```
