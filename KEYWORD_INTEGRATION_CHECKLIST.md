## Common Issues & Fixes

| Issue | Cause | Solution |
|-------|-------|----------|
| No impressions after 30 days | Keyword not indexed | Wait another 14 days, check indexing in GSC |
| High impressions, 0% CTR | Title/description not compelling | Test new title/description with power words |
| Position 15+, not moving | Lower authority | Build internal links, improve content depth |
| Impressions declining | Negative ranking signal | Check for technical issues, refresh content |
| Very low CTR (<1%) | Title doesn't match intent | Analyze SERP, rewrite to match competitor titles |
